The Others

Electrical engineering is a big part of my life but it is not the only part. Here are a few hobbies that I have to pass the time.

Music Instrument

Playing the guitar and the ney, a traditional Turkish instrument, is a huge hobby of mine. I have started playing the ney at the age of 12 and the guitar at the age of 14. The guitar allows me to explore different genres from blues to rock and express my creativity, while the ney's beautiful sound brings a sense of tranquility. Both instruments require dedication and practice, offering unique experiences.

Sports and Exercise

During my childhood, I was highly involved in basketball and track and field. In high school, I continued my athletic pursuits by participating in the school's basketball team and in track and field as well. Although I currently don't have the time to play many sports, I prioritize staying fit by actively engaging in lane swimming. It helps me maintain my fitness levels and reminds me the importance of leading an active lifestyle.

Lego Collection

My Lego collection has been a source of joy and creativity for me. Over the years, I have accumulated a wide variety of Lego sets, from towering city skylines to intricate spaceships and fantastical castles. Building with Lego bricks allows me to bring my ideas to life. Whether I follow the instructions or engage in freestyle building, the process is always filled with excitement and satisfaction. I'm excited to expand my collection in the future.
